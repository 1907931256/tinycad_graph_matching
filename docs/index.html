A plea from the author

Many people download TinyCAD and don't get on with it. Why not drop me an email to let me know what the problem is. I try and fix all of the bugs that I know about. However, many bugs remain, simply because I'm not told. So please let me know and don't assume that somebody else will tell me.

If you want to have an open discussion about TinyCAD why not go to the discussion forum accessible from the TinyCAD web-site.

Often I will fix the bug and email you back the latest version with the bug-fix in.
